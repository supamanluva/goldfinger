# Privacy Policy — Goldfinger

_Last updated: 1 July 2026_

Goldfinger ("the app") is a local, offline party game. The policy is simple:

**The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

- No account or sign-in.
- No analytics, tracking, or advertising.
- No internet access — the game runs entirely on your device.
- The only permission requested is **Vibration** (`VIBRATE`), used solely for
  haptic feedback during play. It does not access any personal information.

Because no data is collected, there is no personal data to access, export, or
delete.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
